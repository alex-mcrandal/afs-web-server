Our Mission

AFS strives to promote sustainable practices across Heidelberg's campus through education, accessibility and promotion of an enviornmentally friendly mindset

Alliance for sustainability is a student-led organization that works to promote a greener living on Heidelberg's campus. Formerly known as the Greenhouse club, members of the AFS now maintain the greenhouse and fishtanks in Gillmor Science Hall. Our largest yearly event, the plant sale, raises money for these projects as well as other sustainbility activism on campus. In the fall of 2021 AFS recieved a $1500 grant to promote the "beautification" of the campus. This led the group to add hammocks for students to study on as well as working towards a new recycling program for the campus. Our mission is to promote green living at Heidelberg through nature, appreciation, hands-on learning, and actvism.
